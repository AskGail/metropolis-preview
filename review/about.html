Our story

It started in Downtown LA.

Dr. James Y. Wang opened the first Metropolis office in Downtown Los Angeles in 2019, after completing his fellowship at Memorial Sloan Kettering. Patients asked for more of it, and in 2022 Brentwood followed. Today Metropolis is five offices across Los Angeles, Orange County, and the Bay Area, still run on the same idea: dermatology that feels different, and treats everyone well.

Why we exist

"To create a memorable, extraordinary experience for our diverse patients, with premium service, advanced medical expertise, and the newest, most effective technologies." Metropolis Dermatology, in its own words

Founder here, de-risk intact
