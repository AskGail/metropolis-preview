About Dr. Wang

Dr. James Y. Wang is the founder and Medical Director of Metropolis Dermatology. He is also a board-certified dermatopathologist, trained not only to treat the skin but to diagnose it at the cellular level. That dual lens informs how Metropolis plans care: precisely, and often by combining treatments in ways a single-focus practice cannot.

Born in Taipei and raised in Rochester, New York, he completed his undergraduate degree in biology at Washington University in St. Louis, graduating summa cum laude and Phi Beta Kappa. He earned his MD from Harvard Medical School and, concurrently, an MBA from Harvard Business School. He completed his dermatology residency at UCLA and a dermatopathology fellowship at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center and Weill Cornell. He speaks English and Mandarin Chinese.
